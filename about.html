about us

Leveraging on the rich industry experience, Sensoheat Engineering Equipment's (I) Pvt. Ltd is acknowledged as a distinguished manufacturer and supplier of a commendable array of varied Industrial Heating systems. Our catered array compromises Flux Baking Ovens, Electrode Baking Ovens, Portable Electrode Baking Ovens, Transformer Baking Ovens, Varnish Curing Ovens, Ovens for Rubber & Plastic Industries, Trolley type Ovens, Walk in Ovens, Dual Chamber Ovens, Conveyor Ovens, Customized Ovens, Industrial Heaters, Tubular Heaters, Band Heaters, Ceramic Band Heaters, Laboratory & Scientific Instruments, Industrial Heater Banks & Duct Heaters, Laboratory & Vacuum Ovens. Along with this, we also offer Temperature Sensors, Industrial & Laboratory Furnace and Teflon/Fiber glass wires. All our products are developed with utmost precision using quality-approved components which are procured from certified vendors of the market. In order to make the associated clients completely satisfied, we offer our product-line in different sizes, grades and specifications. Our products have attained enough popularity amongst the clients following to their attributes including energy efficiency, reliability, optimum performance and compact designs. The prime application of our offered range is in Chemical and Electra-technical Industries.

We are well supported by a state-of-the-art infrastructure facility that plays a vital part in accomplishing all the trade related tasks with ease. This facility is updated with latest machines and technologies which are required for manufacturing the offered range in bulk quantity. The facility is managed and controlled by an efficient team of professionals appointed by us.
